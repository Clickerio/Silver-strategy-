What is SILVER?

SILVER is a market-driven token experiment using a transparent treasury mechanism and spot market buys. No artificial yield, no minting, no hidden incentives.

Mechanism

10% of creator fees are allocated to the treasury. Treasury funds are used for spot market purchases of SILVER. All actions are visible on-chain.
